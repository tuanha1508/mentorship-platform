For Mentors

As a mentor, you'll have access to a dedicated dashboard where you can:

  • View and manage your mentee connections
  • Respond to connection requests
  • Set your availability for mentorship sessions
  • Track your mentees' progress
  • Build your profile to showcase your expertise and experience

For Mentees

As a mentee, you'll benefit from:

  • A powerful search system to find the perfect mentor
  • The ability to view mentor profiles and expertise
  • Tools to track your learning journey
  • A personalized dashboard to manage your mentorship relationship
  • Goal-setting features to ensure productive mentorship
